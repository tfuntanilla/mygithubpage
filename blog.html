    Summer 2015

    7/6/2015

    I spent the last week of June in Big Island, HI, and that would most likely be my last vacation trip for this year's summer. Next week, I'll be starting my first post-college job. While I'm already used to the idea of working 40 hours a week during summertime (I spent my last two summers working full-time), I still feel like I'm going to greatly miss school downtime. I'll still have vacation days, but they're not like school breaks. I'll have to carefully plan how I'll be using my limited time off throughout the year. Knowing well in advance the exact start and end dates of my winter, spring, and summer breaks actually made planning vacation trips easier.

    Anyway, I thought I'd share pictures of some of the beautiful spots I visited while I was in the Big Island. I like traveling and beaches. I thoroughly enjoyed the warm waters and the big waves - Hawaii just became one of my favorite U.S. states for this reason. Hopefully, I can visit Honolulu and Maui next time.
