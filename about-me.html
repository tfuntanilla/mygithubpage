Hello! I'm Trisha Funtanilla. I'm a senior undergraduate at UC Davis, studying Computer Science and Engineering. I'm finishing up my last year of college, and I'm really excited about graduating this coming June 2015. I aim to get a software engineering career started after graduation preferably somewhere in the Silicon Valley because of the opportunities, but I'm open to anywhere. I'm also interested in eventually getting a Master's degree, hopefully within five years after graduation.

Here, I'll be sharing some of my interests and the work that I do. My interests are mostly in software engineering, web and mobile application development, computer graphics, data visualization, and technology-related things. I like learning new technologies especially when it comes to software development.

My non-technical interests include music. I listen to all kinds of music, doesn't matter what genre or language it is in as long as it sounds pleasant to me. I'm always equipped with earphones except in situations when I can't have them on. I also like to travel and explore new places. I've always been interested in many different cultures aside from my own, and I hope to learn more about those cultures through traveling.

Feel free to contact me about anything via email or the comment section below.
